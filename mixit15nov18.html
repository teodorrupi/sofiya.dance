New Performance - Awoken

15 November

On 15th November, my students and I are going to participate in a competition, Mix It! Open Stage in Berlin. The piece will be a blend of theater and dance, and we will be using several visual techniques. The piece is focusing on the core meaning of dance, namely the emotion as the most important part in dance. We will present the topic by taking the part of “dance zombies” that are woken up by the sound of music in the sense that the music should inspire the dancer to move, rather than the other way around. Come and support us!

Details:

Date: 15.11.2018
Time: 19:00
Entrance: Free
Address: FREIRAUM im Studentenhaus, Hardenbergstraße 35, 10623 Berlin, Germany

See you there,
